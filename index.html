The Choice Within

Welcome to the music business kid. You finally made it to the big stage. You hit a million followers on Delayagram, hit 100 million streams on Flopify & PearMusic, and built a solid fan base. Now you have some choices to make. You can stay independent and keep slowly building, but it won't be easy as you've already seen. You'll encounter barriers and trials that will test your inner artistry. ALAS there is another solution! You can sign with a music label and they'll knock down all those barriers and trials will be a thing of the past. Both come at a cost though. Stay true to yourself and art or sell your soul and become a music icon? What will you choose?
